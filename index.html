MY FOOTBALL JOURNEY

My name is Raduk Crispine Onyango. I started playing football at a tender age where I was a rugby player playing for Shamas Rugby Foundation led by Coach Will and his assistant Coach Eduardo.

I came to realize that I had a bigger potential in football, that's where I moved from playing rugby and joined football in the year 2011.

My first football team to join was called Santos led by Coach Rex. He himself saw that I had good talent in football and that football could take me very far, but he always emphasized one thing: Discipline.

2011

Santos FC

Started my football journey under Coach Rex who mentored me for four years, emphasizing discipline as the key to success.

2015

NACOFA

Joined Nairobi County Football Academy (NACOFA), one of the best academies in Nairobi. Our home ground was City Stadium, sponsored by former Nairobi Governor Dr. Evans Odhiambo Kidero.

Under 15s

County Tournament

Selected to play for under 15s in a county tournament. Scouted as a talented Center Back Defender and invited for training surveys.

2020

Black Berets FC

Joined the Administrative Police Team - Black Berets FC, playing alongside police officers, which was an unexpected but valuable experience.

Present

Phantom FC

Currently playing for Phantom FC, continuing my passion for football and striving for excellence on and off the field.
